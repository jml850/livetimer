In some cultures, the more years one has lived, the wiser and more revered they are. In others, however, youth is everything.

Some say that age is just a number. However, the reality of age often shows itself in our bodies and minds. There are certain physical conditions that are brought on by aging that many people might not be aware of, such as arthritis. Arthritis is an inflammation of the joints that can cause pain and stiffness in the joints. As we age, our skin becomes thinner and less elastic because it doesn't produce collagen as efficiently as it once did.

At a certain point in our lives, we begin to evaluate the age we have now achieved. For some, this realization may come as soon as high school graduation, while others may not face it until they are receiving retirement benefits. Regardless of when the realization is made, there are many undeniable truths that become clear with age. People who have achieved a certain age develop a sense that time is running out and may start making decisions based on this eventuality.
